30 second  gameplay description
Tacos fly at you from the sky while you try to catch them in your mouth and eat them.
Avoid barbed wire.
Lose 5 tacos, you get beat up by cholos and game over.
Get 30 tacos you win salsa power up, makes you immune to barbed wire. 
Max score gets in the leaderboard.
